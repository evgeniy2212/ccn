CCN Privacy Policy

Currency Control Network Inc. is pleased that you have visited our website, CCN-Systems.com, and are interested in our company and services. It is very important to us to protect your personal data as we handle it throughout the entire business process.

Security of your information.

To ensure the confidentiality of your personal information, we developed a number of special protection methods. One of our main methods involves storing all personal information is on servers that do not have internet connection, which prevents illegal copying or removal of this information by unauthorized persons via the internet. In addition, monthly payments for our services are set up so that we do not need to store your personal data in our computer system.
We are always continuing to improve our security system so that your personal information is protected in a secure and dignified manner.

Do we use ''cookies''?

We use cookies to understand and save our user's information for future visits.

As is true of most websites, we gather certain information automatically and store it in log files.  This information may include Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, operating system, date/time stamp and/or clickstream data.  We use this information gathered about you from our site or mobile application to help personalize search results, diagnose problems with our server, and administer our site and mobile applications.

Third party disclosure.

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personal identifiable information unless we provide you with advance notice. Ours customers’ information will never be provided to other parties for marketing, advertising, or other uses.

Compliance with laws and law enforcement

We cooperate with government and law enforcement officials and private parties to enforce and comply with the law. We will disclose any information about you to government or law enforcement officials as we, in our sole discretion, believe necessary or appropriate to respond to claims and legal proceedings, to protect our property and rights of the property, to protect the safety of the public, or to prevent or stop activity we consider to be illegal or unethical. We will also share your information to the extent necessary to comply with ICANN's rules, regulations, and policies.

To the extent we are legally permitted to do so, we will take reasonable steps to notify you in the event that we are required to provide your personal information to third parties as part of legal proceedings.
